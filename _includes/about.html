What we do

Work Together

Teamwork is a critical skill for all great programmers. Unfortunately this skill is often neglected in traditional classroom environments. That is why our club focuses on group projects, which emphasize communication, collaboration, and verion-control tools.

Build Software

As a club, we enjoy building a wide variety of software, which includes websites, mobile apps, and games. Deep and broad software experience helps us grow as developers and better enables us to build improved software.

Learn New Things

Our club is a learning experience for members of all skill levels, from total beginners to expert programmers. We won't teach you how to code. Instead, we provide motivation, information, and support for each other to self-learn effectively.
