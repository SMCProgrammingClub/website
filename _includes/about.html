What we do

Work Together

Teamwork is a crucial skill for programmers that many students don't get from a classroom. In our club, we work on projects in small teams, utilizing communication, collaboration, and versioning tools to achieve more as a team than we could alone.

Build Software

Learn New Things

One of our goals is to make the club a learning experience for members of all skill levels, from total beginners to expert programmers. We won't teach you how to code. Instead, we provide motivation, information, and support for each other to self-learn effectively.
