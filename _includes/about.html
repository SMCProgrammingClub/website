About

Our club meets in BUS 201 on Tuesdays at 11:15-12:35.

Work Together

Teamwork is a critical skill for any programmer. Unfortunately it's not often taught in the average CS class. That is why our club focuses on group projects, which emphasize communication and collaboration.

Build Software

We want to write programs we can really use, whether they're for education, productivity, or just for fun. Our members can expect to contribute to websites, mobile apps, games, and other projects that they can be proud of.

Learn New Things

Our club is a learning experience for members of all skill levels, from total beginners to expert programmers. We won't teach you how to code. Instead, we provide motivation, information, and support for each other to self-learn effectively.
